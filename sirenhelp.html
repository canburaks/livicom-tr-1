Description
Livi Siren (hereinafter referred to as the siren) is designed for sound notification in case of emergency (robbery, fire, accident).

Duration and loudness of sound notifications is customizable. The site owner may turn on a setting of the guard enabling / disabling sound confirmation. External LED indicator of the guard state can be connected to the siren.

Binding the siren to Livi Smart Hub
The siren must be kept in a room unpacked for at least 4 hours before any manipulations if it was transported or stored at low temperatures.
Only the owner of the site can bind the siren to the hub. Disable the guard and check whether the hub is online before binding.
Follow these steps to bind the siren to the hub:

1. Open the siren enclosure: loosen the screw (2) at the bottom of the enclosure using a crosshead screwdriver. Pull the lid up to open the enclosure.

2. Switch the siren to the binding mode: pull out protective films that come out of battery compartments. The indicator (1) will blink blue (for 60 seconds) once the siren is switched to the binding mode.

3. In the Livicom app open the "Devices" screen, in the upper right corner of the screen tap "+" and select "Add Device".

The app will compile a list of devices available for binding.

4. Select the siren from the list and make sure that the siren serial number in the app matches the number on the siren enclosure.

5. Tap CONTINUE and follow on-screen instructions in the app to bind the siren to the hub.

The indicator (1) will blink green 5 times after successful binding.
